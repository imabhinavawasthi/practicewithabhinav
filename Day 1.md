### Topics to be covered:
- Binary Exponentiation
- Compute x^n using only O(logn) 
- Compute x^n mod m
- Euclidean algorithm for computing the greatest common divisor (GCD)
- Properties of GCD/LCM
- Linear Diophantine Equation
